## About Me

I'm a 49-year-old developer who grew up in the Atari and Nintendo era ‚Äî the kind of kid who took things apart just to see how they worked. That curiosity turned into 30+ years in tech, from climbing corporate ladders to surviving four layoffs that taught me the same lesson every time: nobody else is going to build the stability your family needs.

So I stopped waiting for permission and started building on my own.

**SquigAI** is my one-person operation. I design, develop, test, and ship every product myself. The company carries my oldest son's nickname ‚Äî "Squig" ‚Äî because everything I build is ultimately for him, his younger brother, and his little sister. Our son has Level 2 autism, and raising him has fundamentally shaped how I think about design, accessibility, and what "intuitive" really means.

> _"Technology should work for you, not the other way around."_
